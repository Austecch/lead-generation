# Event Budget Calculator Guide
## How to Plan, Track, and Stay Within Your Budget

**By Emily Experience**

Creating a realistic budget is the foundation of successful event planning. This guide will help you understand typical costs, allocate funds effectively, and avoid common budget pitfalls.

## 💰 Understanding Event Costs

### Cost Categories Breakdown

**For a typical event, budget allocation usually looks like:**

| Category | Percentage | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Venue | 35-45% | Often the largest expense |
| Catering | 20-30% | Per-person pricing |
| Photography/Videography | 10-15% | One-time service |
| Flowers & Décor | 8-12% | Can vary dramatically |
| Attire | 5-10% | Often underestimated |
| Entertainment | 5-10% | DJ, band, etc. |
| Stationery | 2-4% | Invitations, programs |
| Transportation | 2-4% | Guest shuttles, etc. |
| Favors & Gifts | 2-3% | Optional but nice |
| Miscellaneous | 5-10% | Buffer for surprises |

## 💡 Money-Saving Strategies

### Venue Savings
- **Choose off-peak dates** – Friday or Sunday vs. Saturday
- **Consider off-season** – Winter weddings cost less
- **All-inclusive venues** – Bundled services often save money
- **Non-traditional venues** – Restaurants, galleries, parks
- **Morning or brunch events** – Breakfast costs less than dinner

### Catering Savings
- **Buffet vs. plated** – Saves on service staff
- **Limited bar** – Beer and wine only vs. full bar
- **Seasonal menu** – In-season ingredients cost less
- **Food stations** – Can be cheaper than full dinner
- **Cake alternatives** – Dessert bars, cupcakes, donuts

### Décor Savings
- **Choose a naturally beautiful venue** – Less decoration needed
- **DIY strategically** – Focus on high-impact items
- **Rent vs. buy** – For items you'll use once
- **Repurpose ceremony décor** – Move flowers to reception
- **Candles and lighting** – Big impact, lower cost

### Vendor Savings
- **Book early** – Some vendors offer early booking discounts
- **Package deals** – Bundling services often saves money
- **Hire local** – Avoid travel fees
- **Newer vendors** – May offer lower rates to build portfolio
- **Referral discounts** – Ask about referral programs

## 🚨 Hidden Costs to Watch For

### Often Forgotten Expenses:

**Attire & Beauty:**
- Alterations ($200-$500+)
- Accessories (shoes, jewelry, undergarments)
- Hair and makeup trial
- Groom's accessories

**Venue & Rentals:**
- Setup and breakdown fees
- Overtime charges
- Cake cutting fee ($2-$5 per person)
- Corkage fee ($10-$25 per bottle)
- Service charges (18-22%)
- Gratuity

**Stationery:**
- Postage (invitations can be heavy!)
- RSVP card postage
- Thank you card postage
- Calligraphy (if desired)

**Legal & Administrative:**
- Marriage license ($50-$150)
- Event insurance ($150-$300)
- Permit fees (for outdoor/public spaces)
- Parking fees for vendors

**Day-Of Expenses:**
- Vendor meals ($20-$50 per vendor)
- Emergency kit supplies
- Tips for vendors
- Transportation for wedding party
- Hotel rooms for wedding night

## 💳 Payment Strategy

### Recommended Payment Schedule:

**12+ Months Before:**
- Venue deposit (25-50%)
- Save 10% of total budget each month

**6-9 Months Before:**
- Photographer deposit (25-50%)
- Caterer deposit (25%)
- Florist deposit (25-50%)

**3-6 Months Before:**
- Finalize headcount for caterer
- Remaining vendor deposits
- Attire purchases

**1-2 Months Before:**
- Final payments due (check contracts)
- Vendor tips (prepare cash envelopes)

**Day Of:**
- Final vendor tips
- Emergency cash on hand ($500-$1,000)

## 🎯 Budget Tracking Tips

### Use the 50/30/20 Rule:
- **50%** Must-haves (venue, catering, core vendors)
- **30%** Nice-to-haves (upgrades, extras)
- **20%** Buffer (emergencies, forgotten items)

### Tracking Methods:
1. **Spreadsheet** – Excel or Google Sheets
2. **Budget apps** – Mint, YNAB, EveryDollar
3. **Wedding-specific apps** – WeddingWire, The Knot
4. **Emily Experience platform** – Built-in budget tracker

### Monthly Check-Ins:
- Review spending vs. budget
- Update estimates as you get quotes
- Adjust categories as needed
- Celebrate when you come in under budget!

## 🎁 Where to Splurge vs. Save

### Worth Splurging On:
- **Photography** – Lasts forever, can't re-do
- **Catering** – Guests remember the food
- **Venue** – Sets the entire tone
- **Music** – Makes or breaks the energy

### Okay to Save On:
- **Favors** – Many guests leave them behind
- **Printed programs** – Most people don't read them
- **Extensive florals** – Focus on key areas
- **Limousines** – Uber/cars work fine
- **Save-the-dates** – Email versions are fine

## 🔧 Budget Tools & Templates

### Recommended Formulas:

**Quick Estimate:**
```
Total Budget = (Number of Guests × Cost per Person) + Fixed Costs
```

**Cost per Person Guidelines:**
- Budget: $50-$100
- Mid-Range: $100-$200
- Luxury: $200-$400+

**Buffer Calculation:**
```
Buffer Amount = Total Budget × 0.15 (15%)
```
