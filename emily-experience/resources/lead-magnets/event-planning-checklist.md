# The Ultimate Event Planning Checklist
## Your Step-by-Step Guide to Planning the Perfect Event

**By Emily Experience**

Congratulations on downloading your free Event Planning Checklist! This comprehensive guide will walk you through every step of planning your perfect event, from 12 months out to the day of.

---

## 📅 12+ Months Before

### Initial Planning
- [ ] **Set your budget** – Determine your total available funds
- [ ] **Define your event vision** – What atmosphere do you want to create?
- [ ] **Create a preliminary guest list** – Estimate headcount
- [ ] **Choose potential dates** – Have 2-3 options ready
- [ ] **Research venues** – Visit 3-5 locations
- [ ] **Book your venue** – Sign contract and pay deposit

### Vendor Research
- [ ] **Research photographers** – Review portfolios, check availability
- [ ] **Research caterers** – Schedule tastings, review menus
- [ ] **Research entertainment** – DJs, bands, or other entertainment
- [ ] **Research florists** – Review styles and pricing

---

## 📅 9-11 Months Before

### Book Key Vendors
- [ ] **Book photographer** – Sign contract, discuss shot list
- [ ] **Book caterer** – Finalize menu, confirm headcount range
- [ ] **Book entertainment** – Sign contract, confirm details
- [ ] **Book officiant** – If applicable

### Design & Décor
- [ ] **Choose color scheme** – Select 2-3 main colors
- [ ] **Create mood board** – Collect inspiration images
- [ ] **Meet with florist** – Discuss arrangements and vision
- [ ] **Book florist** – Sign contract

---

## 📅 6-8 Months Before

### Attire & Appearance
- [ ] **Shop for attire** – Begin looking for outfits
- [ ] **Schedule fittings** – Allow time for alterations
- [ ] **Book hair and makeup** – Schedule trial sessions

### Guest Management
- [ ] **Finalize guest list** – Get final headcount
- [ ] **Collect addresses** – Update contact information
- [ ] **Create wedding website** – Share details with guests
- [ ] **Book hotel room blocks** – For out-of-town guests

### Additional Details
- [ ] **Order invitations** – Allow 4-6 weeks for printing
- [ ] **Plan transportation** – For event day
- [ ] **Book rehearsal dinner venue** – If applicable

---

## 📅 4-5 Months Before

### Finalize Details
- [ ] **Send save-the-dates** – Especially for destination events
- [ ] **Menu tasting** – Finalize food selections
- [ ] **Cake tasting** – Order cake or dessert
- [ ] **Rentals meeting** – Tables, chairs, linens, etc.

### Guest Experience
- [ ] **Create registry** – If applicable
- [ ] **Book transportation** – For guests if needed
- [ ] **Plan welcome bags** – For out-of-town guests

---

## 📅 2-3 Months Before

### Communication
- [ ] **Send invitations** – Allow 6-8 weeks for RSVPs
- [ ] **Follow up on RSVPs** – Track responses
- [ ] **Create seating chart** – If applicable
- [ ] **Finalize headcount** – Confirm with caterer

### Final Preparations
- [ ] **Final dress fitting** – Bring shoes and accessories
- [ ] **Schedule final venue walkthrough** – Confirm layout
- [ ] **Create timeline** – Hour-by-hour schedule
- [ ] **Obtain permits** – If required for event type

---

## 📅 1 Month Before

### Confirm Everything
- [ ] **Final vendor confirmations** – Call all vendors
- [ ] **Review contracts** – Double-check details
- [ ] **Create day-of emergency kit** – Essentials for the day
- [ ] **Assign day-of coordinator** – Or hire professional
- [ ] **Finalize playlist** – Share with DJ/band
- [ ] **Print programs** – If applicable

### Guest Management
- [ ] **Final headcount to caterer** – Usually due 2 weeks before
- [ ] **Create place cards** – If using assigned seating
- [ ] **Final seating chart** – Adjust as needed

---

## 📅 1 Week Before

### Final Countdown
- [ ] **Pick up attire** – Confirm everything fits
- [ ] **Confirm delivery times** – With all vendors
- [ ] **Pack emergency kit** – Include: sewing kit, stain remover, pain relievers
- [ ] **Charge batteries** – Camera, phone, etc.
- [ ] **Prepare payments** – Final vendor payments and tips
- [ ] **Rehearsal** – Walk through the event

---

## 📅 Day Before

### Setup Day
- [ ] **Venue setup** – Decorations, place cards, favors
- [ ] **Final vendor check-ins** – Confirm arrival times
- [ ] **Prepare personal items** – Clothes, accessories
- [ ] **Get good sleep** – Rest up for the big day!

---

## 📅 Day Of

### Morning Checklist
- [ ] **Eat a good breakfast** – You'll need the energy
- [ ] **Hair and makeup** – Allow extra time
- [ ] **Pack emergency kit** – Bring to venue
- [ ] **Final vendor confirmations** – Quick calls/texts

### Pre-Event (2 Hours Before)
- [ ] **Arrive at venue** – Early arrival for setup
- [ ] **Vendor arrival check** – Confirm everyone is there
- [ ] **Decoration review** – Final walkthrough
- [ ] **Sound check** – Test microphones and music
- [ ] **Catering setup** – Review food presentation

### During Event
- [ ] **Designated point person** – Handle any issues
- [ ] **Timeline adherence** – Keep things on schedule
- [ ] **Enjoy the moment** – Don't forget to have fun!

### After Event
- [ ] **Gift collection** – Secure all cards and gifts
- [ ] **Personal items** – Don't forget anything
- [ ] **Vendor thank you** – Tip and thank your team
- [ ] **Preserve memories** – Back up photos immediately

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success

### Budget Management
1. **Add 15-20% buffer** – Unexpected costs always arise
2. **Track every expense** – Use our budget tracker
3. **Prioritize spending** – Splurge on what matters most to you
4. **Get everything in writing** – Contracts protect you

### Vendor Communication
1. **Create a master contact list** – All vendors in one place
2. **Share your timeline** – Everyone should know the schedule
3. **Designate a point person** – Not you on the event day
4. **Confirm everything twice** – Better safe than sorry

### Stress Management
1. **Start early** – Procrastination creates stress
2. **Delegate tasks** – You don't have to do everything
3. **Build in buffer time** – Things take longer than expected
4. **Remember why you're doing this** – Focus on the celebration
